ST.PATRICK'S DAY!

St. Patrick’s Day is celebrated annually on March 17 in Ireland and many countries around the world. On St. Patrick’s Day, which falls during the Christian season of Lent, Irish families would traditionally attend church in the morning and celebrate in the afternoon. Around the world St. Patrick's Day is celebrated by enjoying drinks with friends and family. This year though we can't celebrate this beloved day as we used to, as health and safety come first . To help bring the pub feeling home we have created a pub-quiz/drinking game for you to enjoy safely at home with your loved ones!

HOW TO PLAY
